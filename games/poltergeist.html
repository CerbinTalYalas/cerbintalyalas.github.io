Poltergeist

Every year, witches from Alchior land meet for the Blood Moon Night, one of the biggest witchcraft meetings of the continent. This year, the meeting was exceptionnal, and Melene returned amazed by what she saw here. However the delight didn't last long as she arrived in the forest she used to inhabit, and realized the king of Valesia cut down the whole forest. Journey with Melene and Ytia, the poltergeist that never left the witch's side, in their exploration of the kingdom, and help them meeting the King : one does not mess with a Witch without facing the consequences ...

Poltergeist is a 2.5D platform game that gets you on an adventure from Melene's hut to the castle basement, avoiding traps, clearing paths and discovering hidden passages, and using Ytia unique abilities : by possessing several items, Ytia unlocks unsuspected powers. A lantern, and Ytia lighten your path and can set fire to things ; a shield, and the spirit can block harmful projectiles ; an hourglass, and Ytia can even slow down time ! But be careful, the path towards the king is full of arrow traps, rock falls and lava flows to prevent the witch from reaching her goal...

Game Development

Poltergeist was developped by a team of four students from Polytechnique Montreal for the LOG4715 - Game Design course, during the 2020 autumn. Our only constraints were to create a 2.5D platform game, and to design a wide variety of mechanics.

I produced myself the music and dubbing. The arts is the voluntary work of Émy LÉTOURNEAU.
