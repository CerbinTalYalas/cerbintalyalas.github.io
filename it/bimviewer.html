BIM Viewer

Project description

Internship : Summer 2019 - IM-Pact

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of a facility. A BIM is a shared knowledge resource for information about a facility forming a reliable basis for decisions during its life-cycle; defined as existing from earliest conception to demolition. - National BIM Standard.

IM-Pact is a French Start-up providing BIM services, and using many BIM softwares as Revit, Autocad and other tools from the Autodesk suite. The goal of my internship was focused on the recently released Forge Autodesk APIs, and the opportunity for the company to join the Forge community. As Forge Autodesk is still not very widespread in France, the company wanted to position itself among the precursors of its use on the market.

During this internship, I had to develop a BIM Viewer : a web page embedding a viewer for BIM 3D models using Javascript and the Forge APIs, working on both computer and phone. The code of my work is linked below.
