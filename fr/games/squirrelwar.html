Squirrel War

Winter in Montreal is frozen, ruhtless. To survive, squirrels must show no mercy. pCompete to gather as much food as possible, manage your energy and your actions wisely to stockpile more ressources than your opponent !

Squirrel War is a multiplayer versus game in which each player controls a squirrel gathering food to face winter. To succeed, they have to pull toward them some food baskets hanged above te Montreal streets, by running in squirrel cages. However, the more a cage rotates, the more it overheats, and the harder it is to make it works ! Additionally, energy is required to move, run and make the squirrel cage rotate : if running out of energy, the squirrel has to feed from the acorn piles scattered accross the area. The key to victory ? A smart resource management, a clear overview of which cages to focus on, and a constant reading of the opponenrt's actions ...

Creative Jam and Game Development

Squirrel War was developed for the 15th Creative Jam, a game jam organized by Poly Games and NAD Jam in January 2020 : several student teams were competing during 46 hours to develop a game. A theme and a technical challenge were given at the beginning of the competition, and the games were judged at the end of the competition by representatives from Ubisfot, Behaviour Interactive and Ludia Inc.

Squirrel War has been selected as the 15th Edition best game.

  • Theme : Strategy
  • Technical challenge : The more you use an action, the worse it performs.
